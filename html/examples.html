UNCONVENTIONAL INSPIRATION

Internet archives go beyond the domains of large entities like the famous " The Internet Archive", libraries and cultural institutions -They are also crafted by individuals, small groups, art exhibits, museums, academic initiatives, and various cultural endeavors. Recognizing web archiving as a fundamental human need underscores its diversity in practice. Across the internet, these archives manifest in numerous forms: from structured websites to simple Excel spreadsheets, and even as pages within Notion. This eclectic collection aims to inspire by showcasing examples of unconventional approaches  or an archival projects.
